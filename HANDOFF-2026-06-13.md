## The active issue: music UI in Options > Sound

**Goal (Steve's call):** BYO music selection lives in the native game **Options >
Sound tab, below the volume sliders** — NOT in the Mod Config tab.

### What was built
- `MusicOptionsPatch` (`src/BetaDeps.MCM/UI/PrefabExtensions/MusicOptionsPatch.cs`)
  — UIExtenderEx `[PrefabExtension("Options", "descendant::OptionsGroupedPage[@Id='AudioOptionsPage']")]`,
  `InsertType.Child`. Injects a `BetaDepsMusicSection` panel.
- `MusicCategoryMixin` + `MusicOptionsRootMixin` (`MusicOptionsMixin.cs`) — bind the panel.
- `MusicRowVM` (`MusicRowVM.cs`) — one row per `MusicContext`: enable checkbox,
  Shuffle/Sequential toggle, volume slider, live track count → writes back to
  `MusicConfig.Current`'s `ContextSettings`.

### Investigation (from runtime.log + merged-Options.xml, 2026-06-13 10:41 run)
The native Options structure (read from the game install) is:
`Options.xml` → `TabControl` → `<OptionsGroupedPage Id="AudioOptionsPage" DataSource="{AudioOptions}">`;
the volume sliders are the `BaseOptions` list inside `OptionsGroupedPage.xml`.
Category VM type = `TaleWorlds.MountAndBlade.ViewModelCollection.GameOptions.GroupedOptionCategoryVM`.

Confirmed from the logs:
1. ✅ **Panel injects correctly.** `Modules/BetaDeps/merged-Options.xml` shows
   `<ListPanel Id="BetaDepsMusicSection" IsVisible="@BetaDepsMusicVisible">` as the
   first child of `AudioOptionsPage`. `PrefabPatcher: Options: applied 4 of 4`.
2. ✅ **Mixins attach.** `attached 2 mixin(s) to OptionsVM`, `attached 1 mixin(s)
   to GroupedOptionCategoryVM` (×4 categories).
3. ❌ **Root cause — attach-order race.** `MusicCategoryMixin` attaches at
   `t+356ms` but `MusicOptionsRootMixin` (which captures the `AudioOptions`
   instance used to identify the audio category) attaches at `t+515ms`. The
   category mixin decided `isAudio=false` eagerly (AudioCategory still null) and
   cached `BetaDepsMusicVisible=false` → panel hidden. No
   `MusicCategoryMixin: BYO music UI bound` line was ever logged → confirms it.

### Fix applied this session (needs in-game re-test)
`MusicOptionsMixin.cs` rewritten so `BetaDepsMusicVisible` / `BetaDepsMusicRows`
are **lazy** (evaluated when the binding reads them at render time — after capture)
and `OnRefresh` re-notifies them. `MusicUiState.Capture` now also logs whether it
captured `AudioOptions` (or that the property returned null).
